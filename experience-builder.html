Process Organization

Generative research is the core of the D⚡️ Experience Builder. Each project contains details about market segmentation, product goals, business questions, upcoming interviews, user personas and so much more.

Each project follows the Experience Design methods laid out in Your Cup of Tea revealing how the product will serve it's customers.

Methods are broken into phases so that you can flow through the process creating a chronological account of your project. Each method template is flexible; nothing is required and you can undo any action.
