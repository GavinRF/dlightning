Project Hub

Projects are the core of the D⚡️ Experience Builder. Each project contains the team information and details about market segmentation, product goals, business questions, upcoming interviews, user personas and so much more.

Each project follows the Experience Design methods laid out in Your Cup of Tea revealing how the product will serve it's customers.

Methods are broken into bits sized chunks so that you can flow through the process creating a chronological account of your project.The templates are flexible; nothing is required and you can undo any action.
