What's Your Cup of Tea?

Your Cup of Tea: Design for Experience is a guide to the heart of ethical product design. The reader is propelled through the art and science of buliding successful products by accessing the intricate pathways of the human mind.

How We'll Get There

Through compelling examples Your Cup of Tea unveils the profound connection between individuals and the technology they access to reach their goals. Customer experience takes center stage as readers navigate human perception, the psychology of decision-making, and the formation of habits.

Design extends far beyond aesthetics; it has the power to transform lives. Join us in the captivating field of Experience Design, where constraint inspires innovation and technology is a conduit for positive change.

From the Book's Introduction

"We shape our tools and thereafter our tools shape us."
‚ÄîMarshall McLuhan

When people have the tools to formulate their own positive vision of the world, they are empowered to re-shape life for the better. Digital technology enables self-change offering opportunities to acquire skills and access personal development advice. Additionally, digital technology provides innovative ways to organize and present data, enhancing our ability to comprehend and establish connections on a larger scale. As a result, user interfaces are driving radical change across every sector of life, altering our perceptions and interactions with the material world.

In the pages of this book, you'll discover practical methods to tackle real-world, human-centered problems. If you aspire to build a business from the ground up, or to create a product that truly resonates with an audience, first it'll have to meet a distinct need‚Äîbut defining what people need is a tricky thing to nail down.

This book is a roadmap to validating the merit of a problem solution. We'll delve deep into the art and science of Experience Design, a dynamic approach that enhances interactions with right-time, right-place technology to accelerate the way people reach their goals and shape their reality.

It's not just about solving problems; it's about exceeding expectations. The reader will build confidence in their ability to fulfill the wishes of customers by forging lasting connections that extend far beyond a single transaction because making things that people want, is far greater than making people want things.

Products that build connections and prioritize the customer experience will out-compete and outlast their competitors. The products and services that endure are often those that captivate with wonder, have a history of quality, or stand for service. Products like these have a tendency to prioritize emotions and hook into deeply seated human needs...
